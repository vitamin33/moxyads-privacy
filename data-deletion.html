Request data deletion

If you want all data associated with your Meta Business ID or Shopify shop removed from MoxyAds, send an email to moxy.shop.ua@gmail.com with subject Data Deletion Request and include:

  • The Meta Business ID(s) or Ad Account ID(s) to delete
  • The Shopify “Shop” domain (e.g. example.myshopify.com)
  • Proof you own or administer those assets

We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and erase the data within 30 days.
