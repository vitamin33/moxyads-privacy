Privacy Policy

MoxyAds is a marketing analytics tool. We only collect the minimum data required to attribute ad clicks and purchases (hashed email/phone, ad ID, campaign ID).

What we collect

  • Hashed contact info (em / ph)
  • Ad click identifiers (ad_id, campaign_id)
  • Shopify order details (order_id, value, currency)

How we use the data

Attribution reporting and ROAS analytics only. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Data retention

Raw logs are kept for 90 days; aggregated stats for 24 months.

Data deletion

Send an email to moxy.shop.ua@gmail.com — your data will be deleted within 30 days.
